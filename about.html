About Me

Let me tell you a few things...

BIO

I’m Max, a recent graduate and web technology enthusiast. I graduated from The Robert Gordon university with a BSc in Digital Media: Design, Production and Development. During my time at university I completed a number of projects from a wide variety of digital disciplines. Since graduation I have worked on a number of personal and freelance web-focused projects.

While the majority of my experience is in front-end design, development and UX (Adobe CC, Html, CSS, JavaScript), I do also have some familiarity with back-end languages and technologies (pHp, Node.js). I also have a wide range of experience in the hospitality and warehouse industry.

Please get in touch if you’d like to know more about me!

Design

I take care to produce unique and captivating designs that incorporate current and best practices. I am comfortable using industry-standard software to create informative wireframe models, detailed mock-ups and high quality assets for all of the projects I work on. By using a mobile-frist approach I can create designs that work on a wide variety of device types and platforms.

Development

I enjoy working on challenging coding tasks and problems. I use modern HTML, CSS and JavaScript to bring engaging front-end designs to life. I like to keep my skills up to date and often spend time learning how to incorporate new frameworks and techniques into my projects.

User Experience

Considering the user experience is crucial to the success of any project. I evaluate the suitability of a design by considering how an end-user might interact with it. Testing with potential users or creating personas, using techniques such as cognitive walkthroughs and heuristic evaluations, enable me to feel confident in my solutions.
